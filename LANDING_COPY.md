**Q: Is my spend data stored?**
A: Your form data is saved locally in your browser so you don't lose it on refresh. When you run the audit, the results are stored on our servers with a unique ID to power the shareable link — no name or email attached unless you choose to provide it.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**

**Q: What if the audit says I'm already optimized?**

**Q: What is Credex?**
