# ECONOMICS.md — Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Average deal assumption:
- A 10-person engineering team spending $3,000/month on AI tools
- Credex offers same tools at 20% discount = $600/month saved for the customer
- Customer switches $2,000/month of spend through Credex credits
- Credex margin on credits: ~15% = **$300/month revenue per customer**
- Annual contract value: **$3,600 ARR per customer**
- Assume 12-month avg retention (sticky once integrated): **LTV = $3,600**

## Conversion Funnel

```
Landing page visit         → Audit started:      40% (strong value prop, no login)
Audit started              → Audit completed:    70% (form is short, auto-saves)
Audit completed            → Email captured:     25% (after-value gate)
Email captured             → Consultation booked: 8% (only high-savings tier shown CTA)
Consultation booked        → Credit purchase:    35% (qualified leads, real savings demonstrated)
```

**Blended:** 1,000 visitors → 400 audits → 280 complete → 70 emails → 5.6 consultations → 2 customers

**Revenue per 1,000 visitors:** 2 customers × $3,600 LTV = **$7,200**  
**At $0 CAC (organic):** Any traffic is profitable.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**What has to be true:**
- Average ACV: $3,600/year (conservative; larger teams = higher)
- Customers needed: 278
- Monthly new customers needed: ~16/month (ramping from 5 → 25 over 18 months)

**Traffic required (at 0.2% visitor→customer):**
- Month 1–3: 2,500 visitors/month → 5 customers/month
- Month 6–12: 8,000 visitors/month → 16 customers/month
- Month 12–18: 12,500 visitors/month → 25 customers/month

**Is 12,500 visitors/month achievable organically?**
- Comparable tools (Glassdoor salary calculator, Baremetrics open benchmarks) get 20k–50k/month with minimal paid spend
- SpendLens has a shareable result URL — each audit that gets shared (conservatively 10% of completions) generates ~3 new visits
- At 500 audits/day (achievable at month 12), that's 50 shares/day × 3 visits = 150 additional daily organic visits from the viral loop alone

**The math works if:** HN launch generates 200+ audits, 10–15% share rate, and the "AI Spend Benchmark" report becomes a recurring content asset.
